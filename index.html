What is an Ideathon?

First time in an ideathon? Don't worry we've got your back!

An ideathon is ideally a short, intensive brainstorming event held to find creative and efficient solutions to existing challenges. Participants are required to identify any real world challenge and propose a detailed technical solution to it.

To polish your thinking skills, ACM-VIT brings to you (Ideathon 2020), a golden opportunity for freshers to put on their thinking caps and come up with mindboggling ideas! All you need to do is look over the rules and after drafting your idea submit it via the form provided.

Prizes

Nobody leaves empty handed. The prizes include
  • Shoutouts on our social media handles!
  • A direct interview during ACM-VIT recruitment!
  • Winners get to pitch their ideas (and brag about it)

And of course, everyone gets the chance to learn something new and interact with like minded people!

Rules

Here are some things you need to keep in mind while submmitting your ideas...
  • Submissions should be individual
  • The idea should be implementable
  • This event is only for freshers (2020 batch)
  • The theme is open innovation (so basically any viable idea is welcome!)
  • Decisions of the chapter will be final in all cases
